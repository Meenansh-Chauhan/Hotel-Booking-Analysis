# Root Cause Analysis

## RCA 1: High Cancellation Rate in Travel Agent Channel

### Finding

Travel Agent bookings exhibit the highest cancellation rate among all acquisition channels.

### Evidence

* Travel Agent cancellation rate: 27.93%
* Mobile App cancellation rate: 21.56%
* Web cancellation rate: 17.64%

### Business Impact

Higher cancellation rates reduce realized revenue and increase operational inefficiencies.

### Likely Root Cause

Travel-agent bookings involve an intermediary between the customer and the platform. This indirect relationship may result in weaker customer commitment, more booking modifications, and a higher likelihood of cancellation.

### Confidence Level

High

---

## RCA 2: Standard Rooms Experience Higher Cancellations

### Finding

Standard rooms have the highest cancellation rate among all room categories.

### Evidence

* Standard: 23.30%
* Suite: 17.98%
* Deluxe: 16.02%

### Business Impact

Revenue leakage occurs within the highest-volume room category.

### Likely Root Cause

Customers booking standard rooms are likely more price-sensitive and more willing to switch to alternative offers or competing properties.

### Confidence Level

High

---

## RCA 3: Web Channel Outperforms Other Channels

### Finding

The Web channel generates the highest booking volume, revenue, and profit while maintaining the lowest cancellation rate.

### Evidence

* Highest bookings: 15,001
* Highest revenue: ₹443.99 Million
* Highest profit: ₹104.78 Million
* Lowest cancellation rate: 17.64%

### Business Impact

Web channel contributes the largest share of business value.

### Likely Root Cause

Customers booking directly through the website demonstrate stronger purchase intent and experience fewer intermediary-related booking issues.

### Confidence Level

High

---

## RCA 4: Revenue is Driven Primarily by Booking Volume

### Finding

Average selling prices remain highly similar across channels and star categories.

### Evidence

Average selling prices vary only marginally across categories.

### Business Impact

Revenue growth depends more on increasing booking volume than increasing pricing.

### Likely Root Cause

The platform follows a relatively standardized pricing structure across customer segments.

### Confidence Level

Medium

---

## RCA 5: 4-Star Properties Drive Overall Business Performance

### Finding

4-star properties generate the highest booking volume and revenue contribution.

### Evidence

* Bookings: 12,034
* Revenue: ₹354.84 Million

### Business Impact

A significant share of platform performance depends on mid-tier hotel inventory.

### Likely Root Cause

4-star hotels provide a balance between affordability and perceived quality, making them attractive to a broad customer base.

### Confidence Level

Medium

---

## RCA 6: Lead Time Does Not Explain Cancellation Behavior

### Finding

Cancelled, confirmed, and failed bookings all exhibit nearly identical average lead times.

### Evidence

* Cancelled: 30.21 days
* Confirmed: 30.38 days
* Failed: 30.43 days

### Business Impact

Efforts focused solely on lead-time reduction are unlikely to significantly reduce cancellations.

### Likely Root Cause

Cancellation behavior appears to be influenced by factors other than booking lead time.

### Confidence Level

High

---

## RCA 7: Promotional Incentives Have Limited Impact on Cancellations

### Finding

Coupon users and non-coupon users display nearly identical cancellation rates.

### Evidence

* Coupon Users: 20.80%
* Non-Coupon Users: 20.09%

### Business Impact

Current promotional programs may increase transaction value but do not appear to improve booking commitment.

### Likely Root Cause

Customers redeem coupons primarily for monetary benefits rather than as a commitment mechanism.

### Confidence Level

Medium

---

## RCA 8: Revenue Distribution is Geographically Diversified

### Finding

Revenue contribution is relatively balanced across major cities.

### Evidence

Top-performing cities generate similar revenue levels without excessive concentration.

### Business Impact

Business risk is distributed across multiple destination markets.

### Likely Root Cause

The platform maintains a broad and diversified geographic inventory portfolio.

### Confidence Level

Medium
