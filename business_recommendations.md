# Business Recommendations

## Recommendation 1: Promote Direct Web Bookings

### Related Analysis

* Channel Performance Analysis
* Cancellation Analysis
* Profitability Analysis

### Evidence

* Web generated ₹443.99 Million revenue
* Web generated ₹104.78 Million profit
* Web cancellation rate: 17.64% (lowest among channels)

### Root Cause Reference

RCA 1, RCA 3

### Business Rationale

Web bookings demonstrate the highest customer commitment and produce the strongest revenue and profit outcomes. Direct channels reduce dependency on intermediaries and improve conversion quality.

### Recommended Actions

* Offer exclusive web-only discounts
* Provide loyalty rewards for direct bookings
* Promote website booking benefits in marketing campaigns

### Expected Impact

* Lower cancellation rates
* Higher profitability
* Reduced dependency on Travel Agent channel

### Priority

High

## Recommendation 2: Reduce Travel Agent Dependency

### Related Analysis

* Channel Analysis
* Cancellation Analysis

### Evidence

* Travel Agent cancellation rate: 27.93%, highest among all channels.
* Lowest revenue contribution among channels

### Root Cause Reference

RCA 1

### Business Rationale

Travel Agent bookings create higher cancellation risk while contributing a smaller share of revenue.

### Recommended Actions

* Encourage migration to direct channels
* Introduce incentives for agent customers to create direct accounts
* Monitor agent-level performance

### Expected Impact

* Improved booking quality
* Reduced revenue leakage

High

## Recommendation 3: Introduce Retention Measures for Standard Room Customers

### Related Analysis

* Room Type Analysis
* Cancellation Analysis

### Evidence

* Standard room cancellation rate: 23.30%
* Highest cancellation among all room categories
* Highest total profit contribution due to volume

### Root Cause Reference

RCA 2

### Business Rationale

Even small reductions in Standard-room cancellations can produce significant revenue gains because of their high booking volume.

### Recommended Actions

* Offer flexible rescheduling instead of cancellation
* Provide loyalty discounts for repeat guests
* Introduce non-refundable discounted booking options

### Expected Impact

* Lower cancellation rate
* Increased realized revenue

### Priority

High
